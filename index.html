Things I'm sure I'm skilled in
Every single day since my kindergarten I am getting better in these:
Information and Communication Technologies (ICT)
I've studied in Moscow Technical University of Communications and Informatics. Specialty of this university - all sorts of communications (tele, radio, info e.t.c.), so they are preparing one of the best specialists in Russia. Proofs? For example, FSB RF (Federal Security Service of the Russian Federation), Rostelecom, Ostankino Technical Center and Kaspersky Labs actively searching young and talented students for employment. Percent of employed right after graduation students is more than 80%.
Electronic Engineering
Physics and electronics still sounds fun to me. I've mastered soldering iron during childhood, and got a lot of experience in circuit designing. Soviet Union legacy - lots of useless analogue electronic devices to analyse, disassemble and create something new. Much later it helped me to repair tons of different electronic devices. Besides, I am experienced in digital electronics because it makes hardware development a more versatile and simple.
Software Development
Things like PLA, reconfigurable computing, controller - all these words sounds familiar to me. But how can you make digital electronics do what you want and properly communicate with each other or its user? The answer is: Programming Languages. I know how computer/smartphone/microcontoller works, how to make them work the way you want it to work and even not get them "bricked" or burned in progress! Needless to say that you should know computer science very well to write a good, effective code. I do!
